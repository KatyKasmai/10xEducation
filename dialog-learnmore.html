The focus on uncovering the child's passions and interests, and helping them acquire the skills to solve challenging problems through 10x thinking and Moonshot Sprints helps us to shift away from asking children "what do you want to be when you grow up" to "what problem do you want to solve and what do you want to build now".

We are currently in the development and pilot stage and the content is draft. We are working with schools and programs in New York City to pilot and implement, starting with 10x Moonshot Sprints (developed by Team Exponent).
